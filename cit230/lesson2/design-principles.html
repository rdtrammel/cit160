White Space

Interaction Design Foundation

The people at Interaction Design have a beautiful website. Their use of color to guide the user is very well done. Most of the content is in white space, is grouped well, and doesn't overwhelm you.

Then, all of the things that you can interact with, although a subdued baby blue color, come across very bold and obvious. It's very well done.

Contrast

Apple

Apple has always had an exceptional design team. Their website is the first that comes to mind whenever I attempt to design anything. They've also published a design standard called the Human Interface Guide which makes sense when you read it.

Hick's Law

Codrops - Tympanus

Hick’s Law is a simple idea that says that the more choices you present your users with, the longer it will take them to reach a decision. It’s common sense, but often neglected in the rush to cram too much functionality into a site or application.

Co-Drops has a very simple design.
