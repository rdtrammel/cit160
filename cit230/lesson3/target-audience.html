    Target Audience and Scenarios

    Instructions: This section requires some research about the website visitors by looking into the purpose and the industry, visiting similar sites and preforming surveys to discover what scenarios are relevant to the visitor. Develop two personas (Links to an external site.) to describe the target audience for the weather site. This section should describe the target audience being judicious about the selection of the most plausible demographics.

    Some visitor demographic information to consider is age, income, education, experience with the topic and family status. Additional audience information would include where, when and how the visitor will be accessing the site. What device will they be using?

    Target Audience

    1. Citizens
    2. Travel Agents
    3. External Data Miners
    4. School Administrators

    Scenarios

    1. Citizens who wake up to tumultuos noise and wind will most likely wonder to themselves, "What is going on out there?"
    2. Citizens and Travel Agents will want to see what the weather is doing to ensure that a trip is safe to make.
    3. Developers of other apps may also want to use your data to inform users of their apps in more specific ways and they may review your site to test reliability and to see what your website has that others do not.
    4. School Administrators will want to make decisions on whether to close a school or not based on predicted outcomes in weather.
    5. Age demographic will be anyone from the ages of 14 to 60. Demographics in the range of 60 and up still rely on traditional news sources.
